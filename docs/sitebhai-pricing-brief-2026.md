## 5. What a SiteBhai website includes (put on the page to justify the price)

Bespoke, comprehensive, AI-search-ready — not a 5-10 page template:
- **100+ pages** of genuine topical coverage (a dedicated page per service/treatment/
  course, per area served, plus a thorough FAQ and guides). Most competitors ship
  5-10 pages at this price; we ship deep coverage — that's the moat.
- **SEO** — unique keyword-rich titles/meta per page, semantic HTML, LocalBusiness
  JSON-LD, sitemap, robots.txt → ranks on Google.
- **AEO** (Answer Engine Optimization) — FAQPage schema + clear, self-contained
  answers so AI assistants can quote the business directly.
- **GEO** (Generative Engine Optimization) — unambiguous entity info + "best [service]
  in [area]" answer content + llms.txt, so ChatGPT, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews
  cite you. (Delivery matches the claim — the build prompt instructs all of this.)
- Mobile-responsive, fast-loading; enquiry/contact + WhatsApp click-to-chat
- Google Business Profile + WhatsApp Business set up for you (free)
- Live the same night, free hosting, no monthly fee
- Domain in your name if you want one (Growth/Pro include it)

## 7. Why these numbers (market rationale)

- A comparable professional multi-page site in India (2026) runs ₹7,500–25,000;
  freelancers ₹10,000–80,000; agencies ₹20,000+. At ₹2–3k we sat below the point
  where buyers suspect the quality. ₹4,000 clears that and still reads as a steal.
- Google Business + WhatsApp are free tools any owner can set up in 20 minutes →
  near-zero willingness to pay → better as free value-adds.
- The website, custom tool, and recurring care are the can't-DIY, high-value items →
  that's where the price and the tiers belong.
- Recurring care is the biggest lifetime-value lever (Indian SMBs already accept
  ₹3,600–12,000/yr recurring website spend, e.g. Dukaan).
